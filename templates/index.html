How It Works

  1. EDGAR pull — fetches SC 13D/13G beneficial ownership reports, 10-K Exhibit 21 subsidiary lists, and DEF 14A proxy ownership tables from SEC EDGAR.
  2. Claude extraction — Claude Haiku reads each document and extracts every owner, subsidiary, and affiliate with their jurisdiction, stake percentage, and supporting quote.
  3. Entity resolution — name variants are fuzzy-matched and merged (e.g. "Harborview Capital Partners LP" and "Harborview Capital Partners, L.P.").
  4. Risk scoring — deterministic rules score the ownership structure: secrecy jurisdictions, adversary exposure, chain depth, shared registered agents, undisclosed stakes.
  5. Ownership graph — NetworkX + matplotlib render the complete ownership network as a PNG, colored by risk category.
  6. Report — Claude writes the narrative explanation. Every finding cites a specific SEC filing accession number.
